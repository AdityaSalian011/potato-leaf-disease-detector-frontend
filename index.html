ℹ️ About This App

This app uses a Pretrained MobileNet Model fine-tuned on the PlantVillage dataset to detect diseases in potato plant leaves.

Detectable Conditions:

🟡 Early Blight

🔴 Late Blight

🟢 Healthy Leaf

How to use:

1. Upload a clear photo of a potato leaf.

2. Wait for the prediction.

3. Follow the recommended action.
